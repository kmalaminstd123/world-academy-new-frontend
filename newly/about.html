At World Academy, we provide globally recognized certifications, including Certified Professional Credentials, Post Graduate Diplomas, and Professional Diplomas across various disciplines. Our programs are meticulously crafted to be application-focused and job-oriented, ensuring that learners acquire practical knowledge directly applicable to their careers.

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our affiliations with esteemed international bodies. We are recognized as an academically aligned institution by the Society for Human Resource Management (SHRM) and are part of the Higher Education Network with the HR Certification Institute (HRCI). Additionally, many of our programs are acknowledged by the Association for Talent Development (ATD) for recertification credits.

World Academy is also affiliated with Cambridge Academy of Professionals UK, Board of HR Certifications UK, International Authority for Project Management Certifications UK, and CPD Group UK, underscoring our dedication to maintaining high educational standards.
