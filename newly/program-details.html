Program Overview

The Certified Human Resource Generalist (CHRG) program is a comprehensive professional certification designed to develop versatile HR leaders who can effectively manage diverse HR functions across modern organizations.

This program equips participants with advanced skills in workforce planning, talent acquisition, competency mapping, performance management, and reward systems. You'll master the art of HR business partnering and learn to leverage data-driven insights through HR metrics and dashboards.

By completing this certification, you'll gain the expertise to align HR strategies with organizational goals, drive operational excellence, and position yourself as a strategic partner in business success.
